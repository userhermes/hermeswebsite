Schedule

The Hermes Summer School 2016 will take place between 27th - 31st July, 2016 at Cumberland Lodge in Windsor, Great Park, UK.

Cumberland Lodge, our great venue. Image by Dimitra Spathara.

Masterclasses

Hermes 2016 will have masterclasses in materials modelling across a spectrum of length and time scales. These masterclasses will be led by experts who are at the forefronts of their fields in research. These sessions are useful in exposing young scientists to new ideas and thinking beyond their respective fields of study. Additionally, the quality of speakers and the impact of their research has been an important driver behind the success of the previous conferences. For example, speakers at the previous Hermes Summer Schools include Prof. Craig Carter (MIT), Prof. Nicola Marzari (EPFL) and Prof. Sharon Glotzer (Univ. of Michigan), all of whom are internationally leading research in their respective fields.

Workshops

Hermes 2016 will also have workshops designed to develop scientific communication skills. This is expected to be oriented towards group tasks, presentations, industry-related pitches and data visualisation, all of which are centralised around concepts and problems encountered in materials science. For example, the participants of Hermes 2012 and 2014 created videos and delivered (slide-free) individual presentations.
