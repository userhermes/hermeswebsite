Welcome to Hermes 2016

Hermes, an international summer school committed to excellence in materials modelling and science communication. The interdisciplinary conference-workshop program is organised by PhD students and supported by various partners. Continuing in the spirit of the highly successful Hermes 2012 and Hermes 2014 conferences, Hermes 2016 promises to provide an enriching experience for all participants.

Hermes 2016 gives you an opportunity to

Broaden your horizons in multiscale modelling

Attend masterclasses from high ranked scientists

Exchange ideas with other international students

Meet industry experts and get overview beyond academia

Improve your communication skills

Participate in exciting entertaining program

Merge into traditional British atmosphere
